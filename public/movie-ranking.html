The Best Movies According to Allysa

My top 3 movies of all-time

She's the man

This movie is not only my favorite,but also my siblings as well. This is funny and has a good story plot. We memorized every line in this movie.We watched it for several times already. This is about a girl who pretended to be her brother and goes to the school her brother goes to, in order to join the school's soccer team.

Freaky Friday

A famous movie in the early 2000s. This is a good movie to watch with your family. The characters are great as well. There's a continuation of this movie this year.

The Devil Wear's Prada

It's about a woman who applied for a job that is far from what she aims to. She unexpectedly got hired in a fashion editorial company lead by a diabolical editor, Miranda Priestly. Watch how Andy strives to become her best assistant. Another movie with a sequel.
